Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a very yummy italian dish that my wonderful boyfriend and I love to make for dinner. We often make it because it is very easy to make and only requires a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pancetta
  • Peccorino cheese
  • Salt
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil, then add the pasta.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, add the cheese, pancetta, salt and eggs to the frying pan.
  3. Cook the mixture on low heat until the cheese is nice and melted.
  4. Once the pasta is done cooking, add the pasta to the frying pan and stir gently.
  5. When the cheese mixture and the pasta have been combined to your liking, the dish is done!
  6. Enjoy!
